These GAP Days will take place in Porto (Portugal),
at the Mathematics Department of the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Porto [FCUP](https://www.up.pt/fcup).

FCUP is located at Rua do Campo Alegre. Ways to get there can be found at the following [link](https://www.up.pt/fcup/en/the-fcup/campus/map/).

## Accommodation

Among the various options for the accomodation (check the common online platforms) we refer two *** hotels at a few minutes walking distance from FCUP:
 - [Hotel Tuela](https://www.hfhotels.com/en/hotels-en/hf-tuela-porto-en/)
 - [Hotel Douro](https://www.hoteldouro.pt/en)

## Restaurants

There are several restaurants nearby. 

At the [Mercado do Bom Sucesso](https://www.mercadobomsucesso.pt/en/) there are plenty of options concentrated at one place. The 'Mercado do bom Sucesso' is close to the hotels mentioned.

The places referred can be easily identified in the following (google) [map](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/1/edit?mid=1LRTwzXL5WfJJEgd3D9M5LJss8_-CCqg&usp=sharing).

Further details will be made available here at a later date.

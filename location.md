These GAP Days will take place in Porto (Portugal),
at the Mathematics Department of the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Porto [FCUP](https://www.up.pt/fcup).

FCUP is located at Rua do Campo Alegre. Ways to get there can be found at the following [link](https://www.up.pt/fcup/en/the-fcup/campus/map/).
